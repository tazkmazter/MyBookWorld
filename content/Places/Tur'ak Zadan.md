

### **Fortress** Information 

Fortress is about 150 m – 200m square


### **Slave** Quarters  

Housing capacity approx 480 

Slave Quarters Cell Dimensions: 

Each cell is 8.24m x 8.24m. Shelters 20 people. There is probably just a shit or piss pot in the corner or nothing maybe. 

Corridor Dimensions: 2 meters wide. 

#### Building Size: 

The total building size for 4 rows and 6 columns of these cells, accounting for corridors, would be 59.44 meters by 38.96 meters. 

There are 5 of these when the friends first go to the fortress and the wizard and the Zorak are able to construct more as demand requires 

Water is supplied to the complex via a deep ground bore 

Food is supplied by stealing when raiding towns and cities, livestock is sent back. Forraging the dessert. And supply caravans are brough in but it’s never said where they came from
